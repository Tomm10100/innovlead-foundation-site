Key Financial Policies

Policies that ensure integrity in our financial operations.

Asset Dissolution Clause

Upon dissolution, all remaining assets will be transferred to qualified donees as defined by the Income Tax Act — ensuring donations always serve charitable purposes.

Official Donation Receipts

Once CRA charity registration is obtained, we will issue official donation receipts that include our registered name, registration number, and all CRA-required information.

Annual Reporting

We commit to publishing annual financial summaries on this page and filing the required CRA T3010 returns once charity status is granted.

Donation Information

We welcome donations to support our educational mission. Here's what you should know:

  • Tax Receipts: Official donation receipts will be issued once CRA charity registration (T2050) is approved. Receipts will comply with all CRA requirements.
  • Use of Funds: All donations are directed toward educational programs, technology infrastructure, and direct community support.
  • No Salaries: All board members serve on a voluntary basis at this stage.
  • Transparency: Donors may request a breakdown of how their contribution was allocated.
