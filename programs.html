60 minutes Â· 6 lessons Â· Self-paced

Charitable Purpose: Advancing Education + Public Benefit

Privacy literacy is essential for all Canadians, especially vulnerable populations who may be unaware of their rights under PIPEDA. This program empowers individuals and NGOs to protect personal data and comply with Canadian privacy law.

Learning Objectives

  1. Understand the scope and principles of PIPEDA
  2. Know your privacy rights as a Canadian citizen or resident
  3. Understand consent requirements for personal data collection
  4. Respond appropriately to data breaches
  5. Implement privacy compliance practices in NGO operations
  6. File complaints with the Privacy Commissioner when necessary

Lesson Breakdown

1. PIPEDA Basics 2. Your Rights 3. Consent & Collection 4. Data Breaches 5. NGO Compliance 6. Taking Action

Target Communities

All Canadian residents, small nonprofits without legal counsel, community organizations managing donor data, and individuals who want to understand how their data is being used.
